# Sum of 2 Independent RVs
Let 
- $X \sim N(\mu_{1},\sigma_{1}^{2})$
- $Y \sim N(\mu_{2},\sigma_{2}^{2})$
- $X,Y$ are independent.
Then $X + Y \sim N(\mu_{1}+ \mu_{2}, \sigma_{1}^{2}+ \sigma_{2}^{2})$.
